    Our Story

    Born from the
    Mist & Mountains

    Himalayan Brew began with a simple dream: to share the extraordinary flavours of Nepal's legendary tea gardens with the world. Founded by the Rai family in Ilam district, our gardens sit between 1,500–2,000 metres elevation — where cool mists, rich soil, and monsoon rains create teas of unparalleled depth and character.

    Every leaf is hand-plucked by experienced farmers who have tended these gardens for generations. We believe that great tea is inseparable from the land, the people, and the traditions that nurture it.

    ✦50+ acres of certified organic gardens
    ✦120+ farming families supported
    ✦Zero pesticides, ever
    ✦Direct trade — fair wages always

    From Garden to Cup

    The Himalayan Craft

    🌱
    Cultivate

    Organic seeds nurtured in high-altitude nurseries. No chemicals, ever. Just rich mountain soil and rainfall.

    ✋
    Hand-Pick

    Only the finest two leaves and a bud are selected, plucked by expert hands during optimal harvest windows.

    🔥
    Craft

    Traditional withering, rolling, and firing techniques married with precise modern temperature control.

    📦
    Deliver

    Sealed in airtight, biodegradable packaging and shipped fresh to your door within days of processing.
